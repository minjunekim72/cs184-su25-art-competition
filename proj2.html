Oski summer bod

How is the artwork created?

We originally box-modeled a stretching cat in Blender (https://ibb.co/5WXw8rv2), which is why "Oski" has a long tail. After subdividing, the shape smoothed out and ended up looking more like a bear. We also used a custom shader similar to the given one, but with two main changes: we added noise to give a furry appearance, and adjusted the lighting to give the bear (subdivided cat) a brown color.

How long did this take?

This was done over the course of a few days, serving as a break from debugging tasks like 4, 5, and 6. I'd say a total of 4 hours.

Blue & Gold Campanile

How is the artwork created?

This is a Blender model of the Berkeley Campanile at night, with added blue and gold spotlights like they do on Blue & Gold days. I created my own shader to create cone shaped spotlights that go up the sides with some additional ambient lighting. Before the first subdivision, the model has just over 150,000 triangles, and the result after one subdivision can be seen in the second half of the gif.
